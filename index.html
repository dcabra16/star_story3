Star Story 3

PMI Principle 4 - Focus on Value

Situation

In several assignments, we were given options for what to analyze, including case studies, project scenarios, or our own personal examples.

Task

I needed to choose topics that would give me the most value not just for the grade, but for my actual learning

Action

I picked examples that connected to real-life experiences, including past jobs and personal projects. I focused on applying the lesson to something meaningful rather than completing the work just to submit it.

Result

I gained a deeper understanding of the material and could clearly see how project management concepts apply in real situations. This helped the lessons “stick,” and I retained more from the course.
